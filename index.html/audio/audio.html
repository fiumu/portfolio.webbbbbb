Audio Project

    • This audio is a compilation of my morning routine on my weekends. This takes place in my current home where I have my set up with my computer, I used 13 different audio samples. I added a background sound of animals in the morning and started to build the story. The most challenging part was when I put in a new audio, I had to cut it and organize it with the other audios. I solved it by making the audios smaller and renaming every line in the audacity
