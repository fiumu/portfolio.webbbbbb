Animation Project

I decided to animate a really common emotion that everyone has felt before, which is anxiety. My animation expresses this emotion through movement, colors, shapes, and text. I used a simple circle to represent my body before the anxiety starts, which is shown in the first five seconds, and then the movement changes from calm to shaky to show how anxiety feels. The colors also change from light tones to darker red tones to represent how everything feels unreal, and the text helps make the message more clear.

At first, I planned to create a video game animation and even completed a storyboard, but I realized I did not have enough experience to recreate it in After Effects. Because of this, I changed my idea to focus on anxiety, which felt more personal and easier to express. I used position to show the calm movement at the beginning, and opacity to change colors and to make the letters appear progressively. The most challenging part of the project was learning how to use the software, since at the beginning it was hard to understand where everything was, but I solved this by watching tutorials and practicing until I understood how it worked

Software Used: Adobe Premier
